Where to stay

A few options for making yourself at home in Belfast.

Cathedral Quarter

BT1

Bars, restaurants, music venues, and most of Belfast’s best food. Best for guests who want everything on their doorstep. 15 minutes from the venue by taxi.

City Centre

BT1 / BT2

The biggest range of hotels at the widest range of prices. Well connected to everything. 10 to 15 minutes from the venue by taxi.

Titanic Quarter

BT3

A short walk or quick taxi from the venue. Quieter and more residential, with fewer late-night options nearby. Best for an early night or an easy morning.

Botanic

BT7 / BT9

A bit further out, more residential, leafier. Good for guests who want a calmer base. 15 to 20 minutes from the venue.
