The story behind us

Hinge match → Belfast → Berlin → here.

It was February 2021 when Andrew & Tamlyn matched on Hinge. When Andrew realised Tamlyn was in Belfast — a long way from little old Leixlip — he initially said it was too far away…

Seven weeks later, after being serious penpals, they finally met in Belfast. The love story exploded from there.

They spent their entire dating life falling in love, meeting each other’s friends, and making plans to move cities — all of it in Belfast. This is where the spark to move to Berlin came from.

Fast forward five years: Berlin has been our home. From one Eastside to another, we’ve built a life, made beautiful friends, and stayed connected to home. Now it’s time to bring the worlds together.

Venue

Titanic Distillers,
Thompson Dock

One of Belfast’s most extraordinary spaces – and the home of our very long, very loud Saturday.

Titanic Distillers sits inside the historic Pumphouse – the very building that drained the dry dock where the Titanic was fitted out before her maiden voyage in 1912. Now beautifully restored, it’s Belfast’s first working whiskey distillery in nearly 90 years.

The dock outside is 850 feet long, purpose–built for the largest ships ever made. The air is thick with history and great Irish whiskey.
