The Basics

Is it just one day?

The party itself is one day, but most people are making a weekend of it. Belfast is a great spot for a long weekend if you fancy stretching it out.

Getting There

Do I need a UK ETA?

Only if you’re flying direct into Belfast. EU passport holders need to apply for an Electronic Travel Authorisation (£16, online, usually approved within minutes) before boarding. If you fly into Dublin and travel up by road or rail, there’s no ETA required.

Can I fly directly to Belfast?

Yes, although its not the best option. If the flights are direct or cheaper, of course it works. You can decide how you want to plan your journey.

What currency does Belfast use?

Pounds sterling, not euro. Cards work everywhere.

Where to Stay

Is there a group house option?

Yes. We’d like to book a big house in Belfast. It’s the most fun and most cost-effective option. If you’re interested, message us and we can plan a headcount and cost.

Which neighbourhoods are best?

Cathedral Quarter (the lively one), City Centre (the easy one), Titanic Quarter (the closest to the venue), or Botanic / Queen’s Quarter (the quieter one). Full breakdown is on the Where to Stay page.
